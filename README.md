# Beauty Brand Sentiment Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes social media conversations across beauty brands using Brandwatch data and Tableau. The dashboard explores brand perception, engagement trends, and share of voice to better understand how consumers interact with and feel about major beauty companies.

## Objectives

* Analyze sentiment (positive, neutral, negative) across brands
* Compare share of voice between competitors
* Identify key themes driving conversations
* Evaluate engagement across platforms

## Dashboard Features

* **Share of Voice**: Compares total mentions by brand
* **Sentiment Analysis**: Breakdown of positive, neutral, and negative sentiment
* **Sentiment Over Time**: Tracks trends and spikes in sentiment
* **Top Themes**: Highlights key topics discussed by consumers
* **Platform Breakdown**: Shows where engagement is happening
* **Interactive Filters**: Allows filtering by brand, platform, and sentiment

## Key Insights

* Ulta Beauty generated the highest volume of mentions but showed mixed sentiment
* Sephora demonstrated stronger positive sentiment relative to total mentions
* Negative sentiment spikes were associated with pricing and product concerns
* Instagram and Twitter were the primary drivers of engagement

## Recommendations

* Monitor negative sentiment trends to address customer concerns quickly
* Leverage high-performing platforms for marketing campaigns
* Focus on themes driving positive sentiment to strengthen brand perception
* Use insights to guide targeted marketing and engagement strategies

## Tools Used

* Tableau (data visualization)
* Brandwatch (social media data)
* Excel (data preparation)
